# PROMPT.md — Nano Banana Pro prompts for the remaining menu photography

Prompts to complete the dish photo set for `/menu`, in the same house style
as the first 8 images (momo, jhol-momo, mustang-tikka, thali, butter-chicken,
eggplant-masala, table-hero).

## 🛠️ How to use

1. For every dish below, paste the **House Style block** first, then that
   dish's **Subject** paragraph, as one prompt.
2. Generate at 2K, aspect ratio **1:1** (dish cards are square; `table-hero`
   style scene shots are 16:9).
3. Save as `public/assets/food/<filename>` exactly as listed. The site's
   `DishImage` component picks images up by filename and falls back to a
   styled tile when a file is missing, so partial batches are safe.
4. Reject and re-roll any render with text, watermarks, hands, or a
   non-charcoal background: consistency across the grid is the aesthetic.

## 🎨 House Style block (prepend to every prompt)

> Premium editorial food photography for a Nepalese restaurant, shot from
> 90 degrees directly overhead. The dish sits on matte ceramic or rustic
> brass ware on a dark warm charcoal slate surface (near #14181D), with
> faint flour dust and a few scattered whole spices (timmur pepper, dried
> chilies, coriander seeds) placed sparsely at the edges. Single soft
> directional key light from the upper left, deep moody shadows, gentle
> steam rising where the dish is hot. One accent of chili crimson (#C8353B)
> somewhere in the frame: a drizzle of chili oil, a dried chili, or a
> folded crimson linen napkin corner. Shallow, natural texture detail,
> photorealistic, 2K, no text, no watermark, no hands, no people.
